How Does the Big Bass Splash Free Spins Bonus Work?

The free spins feature is the heart of Big Bass Splash and where the real winning potential is unleashed. To trigger the bonus round, players must land 3, 4, or 5 Scatter symbols simultaneously anywhere on the reels, awarding 10, 15, or 20 free spins respectively. During the feature, Fisherman Wild symbols enter the reels — these serve as substitutes for all standard symbols and, crucially, collect the cash values displayed on any visible fish symbols. When a Fisherman Wild lands alongside one or more fish, it collects all visible fish values and pays them out as a single prize. If multiple Fishermen are on the reels simultaneously, each collects prizes independently, dramatically boosting win potential in a single spin.

The bonus also features a progressive level-up system driven by a Wild collection meter above the reels. For every 4 Fisherman Wilds collected throughout the feature, players are awarded an additional 10 free spins and the fisherman multiplier increases in stages: first to 2x, then 3x, and finally to a massive 10x. This multiplier applies to all collected fish values, meaning that reaching the fourth level with a 10x multiplier active can produce truly extraordinary payouts — approaching the 5,000x maximum win ceiling.

  • Level 1 (Base): 10–20 initial free spins with standard Fisherman Wild cash collection. Multiplier at 1x — every fish value is collected at face value.
  • Level 2 (2x Multiplier): Unlocked after collecting 4 Wilds. All subsequent fish cash prizes are doubled, adding 10 extra free spins to the session.
  • Level 3 (3x Multiplier): After 8 cumulative Wilds, another 10 spins are added and the multiplier climbs to 3x — tripling every collected fish cash value.
  • Level 4 (10x Multiplier): The final stage reached at 12 cumulative Wilds. The 10x multiplier is active for the remaining spins — this is where the biggest wins are recorded, including documented wins of 1,239x from a single bonus session.

Random Base Modifiers at Bonus Start

One of the most exciting features that sets Big Bass Splash apart from earlier games in the series is the system of random starting modifiers, which are assigned at the beginning of each free spins session. Players can receive up to 5 modifiers simultaneously, dramatically altering the nature and potential of the bonus round. These modifiers apply for the entire duration of the feature and can stack together to create truly exceptional conditions for big wins. The combination of multiple modifiers at once is what enables the most impressive bonus performances seen in live play recordings worldwide.

  • More Fish: Increases the frequency of cash fish symbols appearing on the reels during the bonus, maximizing the number of collectible prizes per spin and accelerating the overall bonus payout volume.
  • More Fisherman: Adds extra Fisherman Wild symbols to the reels, increasing the likelihood of cash collections per spin and speeding up Wild meter progression for faster level-ups.
  • Start from Level 2: The free spins session begins at the second level of the Wild meter, meaning the 2x multiplier is active from the very first spin — a hugely powerful modifier for immediate impact.
  • +2 Spins: Adds 2 extra spins to the initial award and to each subsequent retrigger level, giving more opportunities to collect Wilds and advance through multiplier stages.
  • More Dynamites, Hooks & Bazookas: Boosts the frequency of in-spin random modifiers (Dynamite, Hook, Bazooka) triggering on losing spins, providing extra chances to convert dead spins into paying ones.

In-Spin Random Modifiers During Free Spins

Beyond the starting modifiers, Big Bass Splash also features three additional random mechanics that can trigger spontaneously on any losing free spin during the bonus round. These mechanics act as safety nets, converting otherwise empty spins into potential winners and keeping the action exciting even when the reels aren't cooperating naturally. Each of these modifiers triggers under specific conditions and is designed to maintain momentum throughout even the most challenging bonus sessions.

  • Dynamite: Activates when a Fisherman Wild lands but no fish symbols are visible. The Dynamite may randomly add fish to empty reel positions, creating collection opportunities that wouldn't otherwise exist.
  • Bazooka: Triggers when a Fisherman Wild is on the reels but no fish are visible. The Bazooka can transform all non-Wild symbols into different symbols, potentially revealing fish for immediate collection.
  • Hook: Fires when fish are present but no Fisherman Wild has landed. A random reel is hooked upward to reveal a Wild symbol, enabling cash collection that the spin would have otherwise missed entirely.

Scatter Assist Mechanics Before the Bonus

Big Bass Splash is uniquely generous in helping players reach the free spins bonus when they come close. When exactly 2 Scatter symbols land on the reels and the third is missing, two separate assist mechanics can activate randomly in the base game. First, a respin may occur where reels containing the 2 Scatters nudge downward while all other reels spin again, giving a second chance at the third Scatter. Second, a fishing hook may appear and pull one of the reels upward, revealing a hidden Scatter symbol. These mechanics do not trigger every time but provide meaningful additional chances of entering the bonus, which is especially welcome given the high-volatility nature of the game.
